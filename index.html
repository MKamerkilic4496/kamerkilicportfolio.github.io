About Me

I was born in Malatya, Turkey in 1996. After completing my undergraduate education in the Mechanical Engineering Department of Fırat University. I started working as a designer in various works of our family company. Then I started working to become a Fullstack developer. After developing some projects in the Frontend and Backend fields in a period of 8 months, I continued my career as a mechanical engineer in Baştürk Cam company for 7 months as a mechanical maintenance engineer due to various reasons. Then I decided to continue my career as a developer in the web and mobile field again.I am interested web part of frontend field right now.I want to improve myself in the mobile field.
